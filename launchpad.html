Lauchpad

Co-working - Events - Classes - Community

The space, which we are calling ‘ The Launchpad ’ is central to our strategy. The purpose is to offer a world class environment that embodies our values of high performance, high integrity, and high collaboration. The design ethos is geared to provide an oasis like feel where everyone who comes is inspired to do their best work and offer their best selves. The revenue generated from leases, events, and classes contributes to our sustainability plan
