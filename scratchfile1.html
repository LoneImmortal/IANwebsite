We offer innovation internships to talented college graduates providing the opportunity to work on innovative ideas with like minded peers in a highly supportive environment. We kill two birds with one stone: build entrepreneur capacity and cultivate early stage startups. During their time with us, they will spend no less than 120,000 hrs working on multiple projects as they hone their skills.

Gain Professional Skills

College graduates are ill prepared for the professional world. Our interns get the opportunity to develop their professional skills by being immersed in an environment that exposes them to real world projects and working with cross functional teams.

Startup Experience

The successful commercialization of innovation requires a distinct set of skills and experience. Our interns are directly involved in the ideation and startup creation process gaining first hand experience on how to effectively validate ideas and create startups.

Professional Marketability

The studio experience places our interns on a promising career path. Upon emerging from the studio our fellows are extremely attractive to prospective employers. It is a powerful way to differentiate themselves with hiring managers while providing them the confidence and credibility in the job search process.

Be Part of a Founding Team

The opportunity exists for interns to be part of founding a startup. Should the project they are working on develop into a startup they interns are eligible to equity consideration in the company.
