The 100 Founders Challenge

A high impact opportunity for successful entrepreneurs to unlock a new generation of founders. We are a non-profit startup studio in Nairobi focused on ensuring young local innovators can access Africa's digital transformation opportunity as creators and owners and not just as consumers
