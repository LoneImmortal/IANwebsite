Impact

Impact Africa Network represents an amazing opportunity to broaden the base of entrepreneurial talent in the world. Mark's vision to create a sustainable organization empowering young African startup founders will benefit the African economy, and creat e much needed high paying jobs. It will also generate interesting investment opportunities in a highly under-served segment of the world for those interested in angel investing. I am proud to support his efforts.

As the founder of Soko, an African Fashion Tech company, I have seen the high potential for locally-grown solutions to foster economic development with the power to positively impact lives. Yet the ecosystem today does not yet match the opportunity, often lacking mentorship and funding to scale. Impact Africa Network is well positioned to train and provide meaningful experiences to youth in Africa, helping to equally distribute opportunities in the digital economy and to compete globally.
Ella Peinovich
