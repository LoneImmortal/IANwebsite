The Solution

Closing the Genius Opportunity gap

Impact Africa Network is a non profit startup studio in Nairobi on a mission to ensure young innovators can access Africa's digital transformation opportunity as creators and owners and not just as consumers.

1. Developing Innovation Leaders

Our structure is designed to equip young talent with the skills and mindset to thrive in the 21st Century.
We provide 12 month innovation fellowships to talented college graduates allowing them to participate in Africaâ€™s digital transformation opportunity as creators and owners.

2. Launching Startups

Startups are the No.1 job creation engine in the developed world . In 2019,there were approximately 3 million jobs created by startups under one year old in the US.
We work on projects with the intention of spinning them into startups. Commercialization of ideas requires a set of skills and experience and we have provided access to mentors and advisors for this process.

3. Building Community

We are continually building a network of mentors, advisors, partners, domain experts, donors and angel investors who provide the necessary support for the work that we do.
